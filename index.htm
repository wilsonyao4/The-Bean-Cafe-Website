What coffee blends are available?

We offer a wide range of coffee blends, including single-origin, blends,

and flavored options. Our menu is designed to cater to all types of

coffee lovers, from those who prefer a bold and strong brew to those

who enjoy a lighter and more delicate flavor.

What pastry options do you have?

We have a variety of pastries available, including croissants, muffins,

scones, and cookies. Our pastries are freshly baked daily, ensuring the

highest quality and freshness.

What services do you offer?

We offer both dine-in and take-out services. You can enjoy your coffee

and pastries in our cozy and welcoming cafe, or grab them to go if

you're in a hurry.
